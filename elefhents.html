About Elephants

Elephants are large mammals known for their long trunks and big ears. They are herbivores and are native to various parts of Africa and Asia.

Types of Elephants

  • African Elephant
  • Asian Elephant

Interesting Facts

Here are some interesting facts about elephants:

  • Elephants have an excellent memory.
  • They can communicate using low-frequency sounds called infrasound.
  • Elephants are known for their social behavior and live in family groups.
